Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 20, 2026

The short version

Snake Unchained 2026 collects no data. None. Not from anyone. There is nothing to opt out of.

The full version

Snake Unchained 2026 (the "App") does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information or user data. Specifically, the App:

  • Contains no analytics SDKs
  • Contains no advertising networks
  • Contains no third-party tracking
  • Does not request location, contacts, photos, or any other system permissions
  • Does not require an account, login, or any identifying information
  • Has no internet connection — all gameplay happens locally on your device

We do not operate a server. There is no backend that receives data from the App, because there is nothing to receive.

App Store data

When you download the App from the App Store, Apple may collect data about that transaction (whether you downloaded it, on what device, in what country). That data is collected by Apple and governed by Apple's privacy policy, not ours. We see only aggregate, anonymized download counts in App Store Connect.

Changes to this policy

If we ever add any data collection to a future version, we will update this policy and call it out clearly in the App Store release notes for that version. We will never quietly start collecting data.
